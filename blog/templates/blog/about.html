Hello, thank you for visiting my about me page on my website. I've created this page to give you a little more background information about myself and to showcase some of the awesome projects I've worked on.

I was born in Holyoke, Massachusetts on March 25th, 1996. I attended the public school system in Holyoke from elementary school to high school, and eventually started my college career at the Holyoke Community College. Even though I knew my way around computers, I wasn't always a computer science major. When I started college I was a Biology major. I had intentions to become a medical doctor, but after completing my first semester I realized that I wanted to challenge myself more. Around this time I discovered my love for computer science. This was a field that was growing rapidly and didn't make me feel like the knowledge I was gaining was limited to a career goal. My second semester I began my computer science degree and started learning to program. What I loved the most about computer science was that the knowledge I gained could be applied to other topics as well. I was not just limited to learning about computer science, but my knowledge could be used to help people in other fields. I completed my BS at the University of Massachusetts in 2018 and am happy with the decisions I have made.

I've been into software development for 5 years and have worked on projects ranging from simple text based games for personal leisure to full scale inventory management applications for Hewlett Packard Enterprise. In my free time I create game play assist scripts for a popular video game known as Runescape, and also like to explore hardware such as high speed drones and custom RGB LED fixtures.

My expertise is in Java and Python development. A vast majority of my work has been on back-end development. For about a year now I've been teaching myself front-end development. Through this I have learned to work with the Django framework, and use some design languages such as HTML, CSS and bootstrap. In fact, that's how you're viewing this page right now!

I love to cook and listen to music when I'm not programming. My dream is to start my own company and help people like myself find a passion in programming. If you'd like to reach out to me for a job interview or would just like to have a chat feel free to reach me at my personal email (omarsanchezdev@gmail.com). I look forward to hearing from you!

- Omar Sanchez II
